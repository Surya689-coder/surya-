Startup Project — MEDQORIN

Automated Medicine Allocator

An AI-powered robotic system that automates the identification, sorting, and dispensing of medicines in clinical environments. Combining computer vision, embedded robotics, and intelligent allocation algorithms to eliminate human error and reduce wait times.

Reduces medicine dispensing errors by up to 95%
Integrates with hospital management systems
Real-time inventory tracking and AI-based reordering
Core product powering the MEDQORIN startup
SOLAR

AI for Solar Efficiency

Machine learning model that predicts and optimizes solar panel output based on environmental data, tilt angle, and historical patterns. Increases energy yield through intelligent panel orientation and predictive maintenance alerts.

GLOW

Glow Roads

Intelligent road lighting system using embedded sensors and adaptive algorithms to automatically control streetlights based on traffic density and ambient conditions — cutting energy consumption significantly.
